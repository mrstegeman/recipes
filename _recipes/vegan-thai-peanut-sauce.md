ingredients:
- 1/4 cup peanut butter
- 1/4 cup water
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 1 teaspoon sugar, more to taste

directions:
- Gather the ingredients.
- Combine all of the ingredients in a saucepan over low heat. The mixture will become easy to combine as the peanut butter melts.
- Continue stirring over low heat until the ingredients are combined and the mixture is smooth and creamy.â€‹ Serve warm or at room temperature. The sauce will thicken as it cools.

---

layout: recipe
title: "Potato Salad"
tags: sides

ingredients:
- 5 lbs potatoes, cut into chunks
- mustard
- mayonnaise
- salt
- pepper
- celery salt
- dill weed
- parsley
- bacon
- 1/2 onion
- splash of apple cider vinegar

directions:
- Boil potatoes until tender and drain.
- Add rest of ingredients, adjusting to taste.
- Cool before serving.

## Ingredients
* Olive oil
* 1 onion, sliced
* 1 bunch turnip greens, chopped
* Scoop of garlic
* Salt
* Pepper
* Red pepper flakes
* Brown sugar
* ¼ cup water

## Directions
* Sauté onions in olive oil.
* Add half of the greens and cook down before adding other half. Add garlic.
* Season as desired.
* After greens have cooked down, add water. Simmer for eternity.

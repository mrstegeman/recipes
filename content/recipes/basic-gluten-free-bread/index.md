## Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cups milk
* 3 large eggs
* 1 Tbsp apple cider vinegar
* 3 Tbsp vegetable oil
* 3 Tbsp honey
* 3 3/4 cups gluten-free flour
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* 3 tsp active dry yeast

## Directions

* Add liquids to bread maker
* Add dry ingredients to bread maker
* Add yeast on top
* Set the machine to gluten-free

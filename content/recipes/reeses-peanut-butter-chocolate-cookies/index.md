## Ingredients
* 1 cup flour
* 6 tbsp cocoa
* ½ tsp baking soda
* ¼ tsp salt
* ⅔ cup butter, softened
* 1 cup sugar
* 1 egg
* 1 tsp vanilla
* 1 ⅔ cup Reese's peanut butter chips

## Directions
* Heat oven to 350°.
* Stir together flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt.
* Beat together butter and sugar in large bowl with mixer until well blended. Add egg and vanilla, beat well.
* Gradually add flour mixture, beating well.
* Stir in peanut butter chips.
* Drop rounded teaspoons onto ungreased cookie sheet.
* Bake 8 to 9 minutes. Do not over bake! Cookies will be soft.
* Cool slightly. Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack, then cool completely.

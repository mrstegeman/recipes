## Ingredients
* 1 cup flour
* ½ tsp baking powder
* ⅛ tsp baking soda
* ½ tsp salt
* ⅓ cup butter, melted
* 1 cup packed brown sugar
* 1 egg (beaten)
* 1 tbsp vanilla
* 1 ¼ cup M&M's peanut candies

## Directions
* Preheat oven to 350°.
* Line an 8x8 inch pan with parchment paper and lightly grease.
* Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
* Melt butter. Once melted, mix with brown sugar.
* Mix together egg, vanilla, and brown sugar.
* Slowly add flour mixture. Mix until well combined.
* Fold in M&M's and press batter into pan.
* Bake for 20-25 minutes.

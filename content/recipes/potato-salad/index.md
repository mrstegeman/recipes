## Ingredients
* 5 lbs potatoes, cut into chunks
* mustard
* mayonnaise
* salt
* pepper
* celery salt
* dill weed
* parsley
* bacon
* 1/2 onion
* splash of apple cider vinegar

## Directions
* Boil potatoes until tender and drain.
* Add rest of ingredients, adjusting to taste.
* Cool before serving.

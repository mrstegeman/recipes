## Ingredients
* shrimp
* avocado oil
* cumin
* chili powder
* cayenne pepper
* salt
* pepper
* tortillas
* mayonnaise
* sriracha
* red onions
* red cabbage

## Directions
* Thaw and peel shrimp.
* Season shrimp with spices.
* Heat avocado oil in skillet over medium-high heat.
* Cook shrimp until pink.
* Serve on toasted tortillas with sriracha mayo, pickled red onions, and red cabbage.

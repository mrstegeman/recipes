From [Recipes by Lily](https://www.recipesbylily.com/toasted-ravioli/).

## Ingredients

For the Ravioli
* 12 pieces Vegan Ravioli use gluten-free ravioli for a gluten-free option.

For the Coating
* 1 cup Panko Breadcrumbs regular breadcrumbs can work for non-vegan versions.
* ½ cup Flour any all-purpose flour will do, and you can opt for gluten-free flour if needed.

For Flavor
* 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder fresh minced garlic offers an even stronger taste.
* 1 teaspoon Italian Herbs consider using oregano or basil for variety.
* 1/4 cup Vegan Parmesan nutritional yeast is a great low-calorie substitute.

For the Creamy Filling
* 1 cup Cashews silken tofu can replace cashews for a nut-free option.
* ½ cup Almond Milk any non-dairy milk will work perfectly.

For Frying
* 2 cups Neutral Oil (e.g., safflower, sunflower) always choose oil suitable for high temperatures.
* 1 teaspoon Salt essential seasoning.
* ½ teaspoon Pepper essential seasoning.

## Directions

* Blend the Filling: In a food processor, combine almond milk and cashews, blending until smooth. Season the mixture lightly with salt and pepper for a balanced flavor.
* Set Up the Dredging Stations: Arrange three bowls for your coating process. The first should contain flour, the second filled with the creamy cashew blend, and the third containing a mixture of panko breadcrumbs, garlic powder, vegan parmesan, Italian herbs, salt, and pepper.
* Coat the Ravioli: Take a ravioli, and first coat it in flour, ensuring it’s fully covered. Next, dip it in the creamy cashew mixture, then finish by coating it with the breadcrumb mixture for that perfect crunch.
* Heat the Oil: In a large saucepan, heat your neutral oil over medium-high heat until it's hot. Carefully add the coated ravioli, frying for 1-2 minutes on each side until they turn a beautiful, golden brown.
* Serve and Enjoy: Remove the toasted ravioli and let them drain on paper towels. Serve immediately with a side of marinara sauce for dipping, making each bite even more delightful.

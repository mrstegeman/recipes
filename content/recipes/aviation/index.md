## Ingredients
* 2 oz gin
* ¼ oz crème de violette
* ¼ oz maraschino liqueur
* ½ oz lemon juice

## Directions
* Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice.
* Shake until cold.
* Strain into a coupe or martini glass.
* Garnish with a cherry.

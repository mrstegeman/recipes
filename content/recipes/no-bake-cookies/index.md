## Ingredients
* 2 cups sugar
* ¼ cup cocoa
* ½ cup milk
* ¼ cup margarine
* 1 tsp vanilla
* ½ cup peanut butter
* 3 cups rolled oats

## Directions
* Bring sugar, cocoa, milk, and margarine to a boil.
* Remove from heat, then add vanilla, peanut butter, and oats.
* Mix and put on a cookie sheet to cool.

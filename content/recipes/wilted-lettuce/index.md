## Ingredients
* Olive oil
* 1 onion, sliced
* 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
* 2 tablespoons water
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1/2 teaspoon pepper
* 8 cups torn lettuce

## Directions
* Saut√© onions in olive oil.
* Stir in vinegar, water, salt, and pepper.
* After mixture is hot, pour over lettuce and toss until coated and wilted.

## Ingredients
* Olive oil
* 1 onion, sliced
* ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
* 2 tablespoons water
* ½ teaspoon salt
* ½ teaspoon pepper
* 8 cups torn lettuce

## Directions
* Sauté onions in olive oil.
* Stir in vinegar, water, salt, and pepper.
* After mixture is hot, pour over lettuce and toss until coated and wilted.

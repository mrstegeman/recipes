## Ingredients
* 1 stick butter, chilled
* ¾ cup granulated sugar
* ½ cup brown sugar
* 1 large egg
* 1 ¼ cup flour
* ½ tsp baking soda
* ¼ tsp salt
* 1 cup chocolate chips

## Recipes
* Preheat oven to 350°.
* Using an electric mixer, beat the butter with granulated sugar and brown sugar until just combined and sandy (do not cream).
* Mix in the egg until just combined, about 3 seconds.
* Add the flour, baking soda, and salt, and mix until just incorporated.
* Mix in the chocolate chips.
* On a large, ungreased cookie sheet, drop heaping tablespoons of dough a few inches apart.
* Bake until golden around the edges, about 20 minutes.
* Transfer to rack. Cool completely.
